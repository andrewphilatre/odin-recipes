Blackened Salmon and Naan with Lime Slaw

Description

Baked Salmon with a smoky seasoning blend on a lightly toasted naan, topped with a homemade lime cucumber slaw and avocado lime cilantro sauce.

    Ingredients

  • 1 lb salmon
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp freshly cracked black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 pinch of red pepper flakes
  • 4 Specially Selected Naan Bread
  • slaw
    • 4 cups coleslaw
    • 3 mini seedless cucumbers, sliced
    • 1/3 cup chopped cilantro
    • 2 tbsp freshly squeezed lime juice
    • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • avocado lime cilantro sauce
    • 1/4 avocado
    • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro
    • 2 tbsp freshly squeezed lime juice
    • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
    • 1 garlic clove, minced
    • salt and pepper, to taste

    Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Pat the salmon dry with a towel. Place on foil lined pan. Stir together the paprika, oregano, salt, pepper, garlic powder and red pepper flakes. Sprinkle all over the salmon.
  3. Place the salmon on the oven. Cook for 15-18 minutes, until internal temperature reaches about 140 degrees. Remove the salmon and let it sit for 5 minutes.
  4. While the salmon rests, place the naan bread on pan under medium heat for just 1-2 minutes per side, until warm and slightly charred.
  5. To build the meal, spoon the slaw over the naan bread. Top with some flaked salmon. Drizzle on the cilantro sauce.
    Slaw
  1. Combine the cabbage, cucumbers and cilantro in a bowl with a pinch of salt and pepper. Toss with the lime juice and olive oil until combined. Set aside until ready to use.
    Cilantro Sauce
  1. Combine all ingredients in a food processor or blender and blend until combined. Drizzle over salmon.
